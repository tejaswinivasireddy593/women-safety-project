Emergency Help

If you are in danger, please contact your local emergency services immediately.

Emergency Number: 112 (India) / 911 (USA)

Safety Tips

  • Always share your live location with a trusted contact when going out alone.
  • Stay in well-lit and populated areas, especially at night.
  • Trust your instincts — if something feels off, seek help.
  • Keep your phone charged and accessible at all times.
  • Save emergency numbers on speed dial.
